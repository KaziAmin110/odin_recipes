Chicken Biryani Recipe

Chicken Biryani

A delicious marianted chicken and rice dish consisting of a plethora of savory flavors

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 serano chilli peppers
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 10 grams of fresh ginger finely minced or crushed
  • 10 grams of garlic finely minced or crushed
  • 900 grams of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 5 grams of mint
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 10 grams of cilantro
  • 1 gram of garam masala
  • 5 pods of green cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp of garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons of ghee
  • 360 grams of Cooked Basmati Rice

Instructions:

  1. To marinate the chicken, combine the vegetable oil, garlic, ginger, chilli peppers, mint, cilantro garam masal in a large bowl with salt and stir together.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and toss together making sure the chicken is throughly coated in the marinade
  3. Add the chicken in a single layer in a pot as well as the ghee and onions and suate the onions untill they are well caramalized.Transfer the caramalized onions to a seperate bowl and set aside
  4. Prepare the rice by washing with a strainer under cold running water untill the water clears
  5. In the pot in which you caramalized the onions, fry the chicken for 5 minutes untill golden brownFlip the other sid and do the same. Once golden brown transfer the chicken back to the bowl you marinated in
  6. Add the chicken on top of the rice, and then the caramalized onions at the top
  7. Add 1 cup of water for boiling the rice and place a lid on the pot
  8. Cook the chicken and rice for 20 minutes
