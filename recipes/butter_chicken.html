Butter Chicken Recipe

Butter Chicken

A delicious dish consisting of savory tandoori chicken and a rich, creamy sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon Butter
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger finely minced or crushed
  • 2-3 cloves garlic finely minced or crushed
  • 2-3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder or paprika
  • 1 tablspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Cooked Rice/Naan for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet pan over medium-high heat. Add the oil, butter, and onions and cook onions untill light golden brown.
  2. Add Ginger and Garlic and let ingredients cook for 30 seconds, stirring lightly
  3. Add the chicken, tomato paste, and spices and cook for 5-6 minutes untill everything is cooked through.
  4. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 8-10 minutes stirring occasionally. Serve over cooked rice/naan.
