Masala Chai Recipe

Masala Chai

A refreshing tea which consists of spices and milk

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Black Tea
  • Spices-cloves, peppercorn, cardamom, and cinnamon-
  • Ginger
  • Sweetener-Granulated Sugar / Brown Sugar / Honey-

Instructions:

  1. Crush the spices - Break up the spaces using a mortar and pestle
  2. Boil the water - Place a stainless steel pan over high head and add water.
  3. As the water comes to a boil, add the spices and ginger so that they can release their flavors
  4. Prepare the rice by washing with a strainer under cold running water untill the water clears
  5. Pour in milk
  6. Double boil or aerate the Chai
  7. Simmer untill Done
